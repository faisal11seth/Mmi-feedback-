MMI Ethics Feedback

This is a UK medical school ethics station. Answer the main question then each follow-up separately.
You’ll receive: domain scores /10, feedback for each question, and rigid model answers (main + each follow-up).

Station brief

Reading time: 2 minutes
Response time: 6 minutes
Follow-ups: 2 minutes
What this station tests: autonomy vs beneficence/non-maleficence, capacity, informed refusal/consent, communication under pressure, escalation/senior support, MDT/family involvement, documentation, and ethically acceptable alternatives. UK-style reasoning (GMC/NHS approach).
Main ethics question
A patient refuses a life-saving blood transfusion for religious reasons. Discuss how you would approach this situation.
Follow-up 1
What would you do if the patient did not have capacity?
Follow-up 2
How would you involve the multidisciplinary team or family?
Follow-up 3
Can you suggest any ethically acceptable alternatives?
